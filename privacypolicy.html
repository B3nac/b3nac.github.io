Apkrelief Privacy Policy

Effective Dec 10, 2019

What We Collect

We only collect email addresses for our newsletter and Android application info that is used to evaluate potential vulnerabilities based on neural network and pentest results. All testing information is for evaluating your application and the test results are not disclosed to any third parties. Apkrelief has no third party integrations to assure you we will not share any information.

How We Use Information About you

At Apkrelief we only use your account information to contact you during the vulnerability discovery, triage, analysis, and resolution process. We will not disclose any information to third parties.

How Information About You Is Shared

No information about you is shared to maintain our nondisclosure agreement with all customers. Since we are a company that deals with vulnerability management and disclose we understand how to keep that information secure.

Ads and Analytics Partners

Currently Apkrelief does not have any ads or analytics partners. If this changes we will notify all Apkrelief customers.

Your Choices

As an Apkrelief customer you have the option to opt out of email newsletters and change any stored profile options.

Other Information

Cookie Policy

All cookies are only used to validate and expire sessions. You can reject third party cookies that are stored in your browser while using our services. Customers will be notified if cookies are used for anything other than validating customer sessions to access their private testing results.

Information Security

The security of your information is of the utmost importance to us. We take various steps to ensure your information is protected against theft, misuse, uauthorized access, and disclosure. Employees of Apkrelief only have access to specific clients they are working with, all reports are scoped accordingly. The only time information will be disclosed is with consent and according to the clients disclosure policy. Apkrelief understands and adheres to the morals of non-disclosure agreements.
